Older Women Coders

Who are we?

Older Women Coders is a group of older women and their supporters who are coding, building, mentoring, educating, and generally having fun (mostly within the San Francisco Bay Area).

Where are we?

You can find us at meetups and on slack.

What are we up to?

We are creating experiences for folks like us.
