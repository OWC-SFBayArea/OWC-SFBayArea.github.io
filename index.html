Older Women Coders

Who are we?

We are older women coders: we see human problems that technology can solve. We joined together to:

  • empower older coders, especially "aged-out" STEM workers
  • establish a channel of visibility for older women STEM workers
  • provide continuing technical education to older women
  • leverage project support, community, and mentorship in the larger hi-tech community -- especially to support women who are struggling to stay in tech professions
  • eliminate the stigma of age in tech
  • promote the understanding that we are not alone

... and serve up a shining example of our BAD-ASSery!

We recognize the inherent value in older STEM workers. We know that older STEM workers are an underserved market, because our own needs are not being met. We are not alone in these understandings.

Where are we?

Based in the San Francisco Bay Area, we have members from around the US and meet online regularly. You can find us at meetups and on slack.
